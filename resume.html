Transformative Experiences

NSLI-Y: Jordan

The National Security Language Initiative for Youth is a State Department scholarship program that immerses students in countries where languages critical to our national security are spoken. I had the honor of spending six weeks in Jordan through NSLI-Y in the summer of 2015, an experience which catalyzed my interest in refugee issues, particularly refugee education.

  • Learn More

MIT INSPIRE

MIT INSPIRE is the only national high school research competition in the Humanities, Arts, and Social Sciences. In the end, I was awarded first place in the Economics category at MIT INSPIRE 2016 for my research on the impact of refugee students on their host-country peers. More importantly, I was inspired by the ingenuity of my peers and the vitality of the MIT research community.
