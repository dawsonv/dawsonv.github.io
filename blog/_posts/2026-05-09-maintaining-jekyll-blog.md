Keeping your blog organized is key to long-term success. Here are some tips on how to structure your posts, use categories, and manage assets effectively within Jekyll.

### Folder Structure
Jekyll uses the `_posts` folder by default. For better organization, you can also use subfolders within `_posts` for different years or topics.

### Metadata
Front matter is essential. Always include a title, date, and layout. You can also add custom fields like tags or categories.
